What's your open rate? +
Our campaigns average 40-50% open rates — more than double the industry average of 20%. We achieve this through targeted lists and personalized subject lines.
Do I need to do anything? +
Just fill out the quote form and approve the email copy before we send. After that, warm leads land in your inbox and you handle the conversation from there.
Is cold email legal? +
Yes — B2B cold email is legal under CAN-SPAM and GDPR when done correctly. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe option and complies with all regulations.
How is this different from buying a lead list? +
Buying a list gives you cold contacts with no context. We build a fresh, verified list, write personalized outreach, and run the entire campaign for you — so leads arrive already warmed up.
